Lady the Street Dog
about today puppies donate

In the first week of August 2016, a rescue volunteer named Ketty found a dog and her five babies living on the side of the road in Puerto Rico. Less than a year and a half old herself, the mama was caring for her tiny, newborn puppies in a hole she'd dug under a concrete basketball court. Luckily, the puppies were only days old at the time so they were in good health. The mother dog was very friendly and allowed humans to intervene. Ketty took the mom, who she named Lady, and her five babies into her home and within no time all the dogs were running around, playing and eating well. The organization that Ketty volunteers for, called Stray for the Heart, put Lady and her babies up for adoption so that they could all find loving, permanent homes in New York City. The rest is history!
